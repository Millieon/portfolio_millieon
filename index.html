Millieon Hu

Millieon Hu (b.1999, Beijing) is a computational artist living and working in London. She studied at University of Amsterdam (2021-2022) and Goldsmiths, University of London (2022-2023). With a data science and computer science background, Millieon holds great interests in creating art with big data, artificial intelligence, and sensors, working across multi-disciplinary practice including interactive installations, screen-based artworks, performances and audio-visuals. Through varied mediums, the artist investigates the notion of Asian diasporas, censorship, linguistics and how our fragility, and emotions could be expressed through technological approaches.
